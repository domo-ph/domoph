How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Domo account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  • Open the Domo app on your device
  • Navigate to your Profile screen
  • Scroll down to the bottom of the profile page
  • Tap on the "Delete Account" button
  • A confirmation modal will appear asking you to confirm the deletion
  • Review the information displayed in the modal
  • If you're sure you want to proceed, tap "Delete" to confirm
  • If you change your mind, tap "Cancel" to return to your profile
  • Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted
  • You will be automatically signed out after the deletion is complete
⚠️ Important Warning:

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will delete:

  • Your user account and profile information
  • All your data and settings
  • Your activity history
  • All associated household data (depending on your role)

Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming the deletion.

ℹ️ Note:

Some accounts may have restrictions that prevent immediate deletion. If your account cannot be deleted, the system will display the specific reasons why. Common reasons include:

  • Active contracts or agreements
  • Pending transactions or payments
  • Administrative restrictions

Need Help? If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact our support team.
