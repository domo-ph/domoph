Frequently Asked Questions

What is Domo?
Domo keeps your house in order without having to manage everything yourself.
What can I do with Domo?
Domo is divided into 5 main sections, found at the bottom of your screen. Let's take a look at each one of them here:
1. Home
   
Start here to see the latest activity in your household as it's recorded, in real-time. You'll also immediately see if there are any outstanding issues to manage or everything is running smoothly. It's quick and easy to spot and helps you to get your peace-of-mind.
   
2. For Review
   
Apart from being able to respond to items in the Home section, you can get things done by task under For Review. Items that need your action, like viewing and approving leaves, payroll, and reimbursements are  accessible here.
   
3. This Week
   
Any work your help needs to accomplish on a specific day goes in This Week. Create a simple task in 2 seconds without any typing at all, or a more complex one that requires multiple steps in checkboxes. If there's anything missing, easily clarify it with your staff by commenting directly on the task card.
   
4. Staff
   
All records and activity history of your staff are kept in this section.
   
5. Expenses
   
Records, including photos of bills/receipts, of your household bills are all kept securely here. You can input the bills yourself, have your staff do it, or a combination of the two.
Can I pay monthly gov't contributions (SSS/PhilHealth/Pag-Ibig) on my phone with Domo instead of going to payment offices to wait in big lines?
Yes. You can pay in-app with your favorite credit card, digital wallet, or bank account. What's more, Domo calculates the correct contributions for each of your staff so there's no guesswork involved.
